First 5 Days

Day 1

On your first day you should set up a tent and campfire to keep warm, then search for suitable edible vegetation. Once you have done that, spend the rest of the day keeping warm.

Day 2

Today, you can try hunting. This should take the whole day, so eat the last of your rations today.

Day 3

Start eating whatever you caught yesterday today. You can hunt some more, or you can walk further afield. After that, you should improve your camp.

Day 4

If you are staying for a long time, today try cutting down a few trees(with a knife) This way you can star to make a more pernament shelter.

Day 5

Day to leave if you are doing this just for fun. If you are not, try continuing the shelter.
